# Voiceover Script

La Ultra: Run & Bee is a daily companion for runners who want to train with awareness, not pressure.

Built by Dr. Rajat Chauhan, the app brings movement and mindfulness into one simple web experience. Start with a daily Feel check-in, then choose the right work for the body you actually have today.

From the home screen, runners can open the day's plan, reset with guided breathing, take functional tests, and review progress over time. The experience is designed to be quiet, focused, and easy to return to.

Sign in with Google to sync Feel, Your Plan, Functional Tests, and Progress across devices, or continue as a guest when you want to begin on this device.

Admin tools are available for authenticated admins to manage users, plans, messages, announcements, and activity.

La Ultra: Run & Bee. Built to connect runners, guidance, and the full training experience in one place.
